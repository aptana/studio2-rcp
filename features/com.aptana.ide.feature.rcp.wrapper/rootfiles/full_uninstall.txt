Uninstalling/Re-installing Aptana
====================================================================================================
If you need to fully uninstall and reinstall aptana, you must delete a few folders that are not
automatically deleted by the uninstaller. Please see the instructions entitled 'How do I repair my
Aptana Studio installation?' at:

http://www.aptana.com/docs/index.php/Tips_and_Tricks#Repair_your_Aptana_installation
